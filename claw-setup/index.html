FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What's the difference between this and a regular AI chatbot?
A chatbot answers questions. An OpenClaw agent runs code, searches the web, controls a browser, reads and writes files, sends messages across platforms, and collaborates with other agents. It's the difference between a receptionist and a team.
Do I need my own server?
OpenClaw runs on macOS, Linux, Raspberry Pi, VPS, or Docker. I'll help you pick the right setup for your use case and budget. A $5/month VPS works for most setups.
Which AI models does it support?
Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (GPT-4), Google (Gemini), local models via Ollama, and 15+ other providers. You can mix models per agent — fast models for chat, powerful models for deep work.
Is my data safe?
OpenClaw is self-hosted. Your data stays on your infrastructure. No third-party SaaS middleman. Agents can be sandboxed with scoped tool policies and allowlists.
Can I add more agents or channels later?
Yes. OpenClaw is modular. Adding a new agent or channel is a config change. I provide documentation so you can extend the system yourself, or book a follow-up.
What ongoing costs should I expect?
Just your AI model API costs (typically $10–50/month depending on usage) and hosting if you use a VPS. No OpenClaw license fees.
